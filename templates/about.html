About RetinaScan AI

A deep learning system for retinal disease detection, built as a BCA final year capstone project and deployed end-to-end on AWS.

⚠️ AI-assisted educational tool. Not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis — always consult a qualified ophthalmologist.

The Project

RetinaScan AI analyzes retinal fundus photographs and classifies them into one of 6 categories, including a healthy retina, using a convolutional neural network built on EfficientNetB0 transfer learning.

To keep the model interpretable rather than a black box, every prediction is paired with a Grad-CAM heatmap that highlights the exact region of the retina that influenced the model's decision — giving a visual, explainable result rather than just a label and a percentage.

The model was trained and validated on a dataset of roughly 1,500 retinal images, reaching approximately 73.5% validation accuracy across all 6 classes.

Python TensorFlow / Keras EfficientNetB0 Grad-CAM Flask OpenCV
